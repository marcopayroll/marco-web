Your entity.

Full HR operations delivered by Marco's in-country specialists. You have the entity. We handle the day-to-day HR so your people are paid, compliant, onboarded, and looked after without you needing a local HR hire in every market.

WHAT IS HRO

The HR team you do not have to hire locally.

HR Operations Support is Marco's service for companies that have their own legal entity in a country but no local HR staff to manage it. Marco's in-country specialists handle the full spectrum of HR administration, from onboarding to offboarding and everything in between.

It is not outsourcing your HR strategy. You keep control of how you manage your people. Marco handles the operational execution that requires local knowledge, local language, and local compliance expertise.

You have an entity but no local HR

Your entity is registered and operational but you have no one on the ground managing daily HR administration.

Your team is too small to justify a full-time HR hire

Five employees in one country does not warrant a full-time local HR manager. Marco covers the function without the overhead.

Your HQ HR team does not know local requirements

Central HR can set policy globally, but local statutory requirements, leave laws, and benefit rules need someone who knows the market.

You are entering a new market and need HR running from day one

Marco sets up the full HR operation before your first hire lands so processes are in place when the team arrives.

HR Procedure and Employee Handbook

Custom HR procedures and a local employee handbook built for your company and your country. Updated when local laws change so your documentation stays compliant.

  • Ongoing updates for legal changes

Payroll

Full payroll service including setup, change data management, gross-to-net calculations, payments, social benefit contributions, and tax filing.

  • Monthly payroll calculation and disbursement
  • Statutory contributions and tax filing
  • Payslip generation and record keeping

Benefits and Insurance

Implementation, coordination, and enrollment into the right benefit plans for each country. Includes government welfare application support for clients and employees.

  • Statutory benefit setup and enrollment
  • Supplementary insurance coordination
  • Government welfare application support

Time Off Management

Country-specific leave policies covering annual leave, public holidays, sick leave, and other statutory entitlements. All records maintained for compliance and global reporting.

  • Country-specific leave policy setup
  • Leave records and balance tracking
  • Holiday calendar management

Expense Processing

Compliant expense policies implemented and enforced. All expense data and receipts reviewed and processed by local accounting teams before reimbursement.

  • Country-specific expense policy
  • Receipt review and tax categorization
  • Reimbursement via payroll or direct payment

Employee Records and Reporting

Employee data and document records maintained to satisfy both your global reporting requirements and local government statutory obligations. Manpower updates to government agencies handled on your behalf.

  • Employee master data management
  • Statutory reporting to authorities
  • Global data integration with your HRIS

Privacy and Data Protection

Data governance framework built to local privacy requirements. Marco's operations comply with GDPR, PDPA, HIPAA, and all other applicable local data protection laws across every market we serve.

  • GDPR, PDPA, HIPAA compliance

  • Secure employee data handling

Offboarding

Structured support for all exit scenarios: voluntary resignation, mutual termination, or involuntary separation. Final pay, statutory de-registration, and equipment recovery all managed.

  • All termination types supported
  • Final pay calculation and clearance
  • Social security and benefit de-registration

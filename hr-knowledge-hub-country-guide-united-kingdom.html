Overtime

UK law does not mandate overtime pay; governed by employment contract (typically 1.5× or Time Off In Lieu – TOIL)
Hourly rate after overtime must not fall below the National Minimum Wage
Bank Holiday work: no statutory overtime pay; determined by contract (typically 1.5× – 2×)

9. Key Regulations

Employment Rights Bill 2025
Major reform: proposes removing the 2-year qualifying period for unfair dismissal claims, restricting zero-hours contracts, and establishing a Fair Work Agency
IR35 / Off-Payroll Working Rules
Determines contractor tax status; medium and large businesses must assess IR35 status and bear responsibility
Auto-Enrolment Pension
All eligible employees automatically enrolled; employer minimum 3% + employee 5% = 8%; can opt out
Gender Pay Gap Reporting
Companies with 250+ employees must publish annual gender pay gap reports (deadline: 4 April)
Right to Request Flexible Working
Employees can request flexible working from day 1 of employment (Day-1 Right); employer must respond within 2 months
Holiday Pay Reform (from 2024)
Irregular-hours workers’ holiday pay may accrue at 12.07% (rolled-up holiday pay reinstated as lawful)

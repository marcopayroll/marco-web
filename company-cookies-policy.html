Cookies Policy

What are Cookies?

Marco uses "cookies" (small files that a site places on your hard drive for identification purposes) and similar technologies. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID to track pages visited. These files are used for site registration and customization the next time you visit the site. This data will enable us to recognize your browser and help us to track visitors to our Website thus enabling us to understand better the products and services that will be most suitable to you.

This Website may include links and connections to third party websites, products and services as well as so-called community plug-ins of third parties. Third party plug-ins integrated into this Website are loaded from third party servers and thus the third party may install their own cookies on the user's device. Your interactions with those third parties and applications offered on our Website are subject to the privacy policies or notices of such third parties. We recommend that you familiarise yourself with those third party privacy policies or notices.

The type of Cookies Marco use

Marco uses Cookies to enable us to improve our service to you and to provide certain features that you may find useful. The collection of data through cookies may or may not be in addition to other information you have provided about yourself or in addition to other information collected for purposes stated in our Privacy Policy. The cookies we use do not detect any other information stored on your computers.

How to manage Cookies

You can manage the cookies and customize your experience on this Website via the Cookies Settings.

If you do not want us to place Cookies on your device, you can disable them via the settings or options of your browser. You may refer to the below links to the official websites of various commonly used browsers:

  • Microsoft Edge
  • Delete and manage cookies – Microsoft Support
  • Firefox
  • Cookies – Information that websites store on your computer | Firefox Help (mozilla.org)
  • Google Chrome
  • Clear, allow & manage cookies in Chrome – Computer – Google Chrome Help
  • Safari
  • Legal – Use of Cookies – Apple

Please note: when you change the cookie settings for our sites, you may not be able to use certain sections of our Website or they may not function properly.

Please note that if you clear your cookies, or if you use a different browser or device, you will be asked again to consent to the use of cookies and/or interest-based advertising, which may require you to reset your choices.

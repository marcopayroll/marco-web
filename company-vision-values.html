ABOUT MARCO

The infrastructure
behind global work

Most companies don't fail at global expansion because of strategy. They fail at the operational layer — payroll, compliance, local employment law. That's the problem Marco was built to solve.

OUR VISION

Simplify Global Workforce

Global work has been unnecessarily complicated for too long. Payroll processed through chains of intermediaries. Compliance treated as a guessing game. Workers in one country paid weeks after those in another. We started Marco because we believed this didn't have to be the reality. It still doesn't.

OUR VALUES

Open.

Humble.

Confident.

These are not slogans. They are the operating principles behind how Marco makes decisions, how we price our services, how we choose the clients we work with, how we protect the people we serve, and how we build trust across every country we operate in.

01

Be Open

Remove the walls.
Connect the knowledge.

Information barriers of global services should be lifted. The service provider who has the knowledge, providing its services, should provide them directly to the one who needs, with a fair and comfortable pay.

There are more than enough social and translation tools to help us reach any person, in any part of the world.

To our best ability, together with our knowledge owners, service professionals, and technology platforms, we should make the information and services provided and paid, openly, accurately, and fairly.

"Openness is not a feature you add. It is a wall you choose to tear down."

02

Be Humble

Every person.
Every culture.
The same respect.

We respect all our employees, contracting workers, customers, service providers, knowledge owners, community contributors, in all countries and all fields, with the same standard.

We respect all government regulations, country cultures, people's values, and the ways how people like to be communicated with.

We respect all our customers' employees' and our own employees' privacy, and their rights of making choices.

"Humility is not about being modest. It is about treating every person, every culture, every rule — as if it matters. Because it does."

03

Be Confident

Know who you are.
Own your worth.
Choose wisely.

We always understand who we are and the services we are delivering.

We always charge the most reasonable fees for our services.

We always have our own choice to work with the parties we are comfortable with.

"Knowing who you are is not arrogance. It is the only honest way to do business."

THE MARCO COMMITMENT

Open in how we share

Humble in how we serve

Confident in who we choose to be

These three values are not aspirational. They are operational. They define the decisions we make when there is no script, no precedent, and no easy answer. They are what Marco stands for in every country, every currency, every conversation.

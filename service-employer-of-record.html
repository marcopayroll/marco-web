SERVICES  ·  EMPLOYER OF RECORD

Hire anywhere.
Entity not required.

Marco is the legal employer. You keep full operational control. Hire and pay staff in any country without setting up a local entity. Marco handles employment contracts, payroll, statutory benefits, and HR operations on your behalf, in full local compliance.

WHAT IS EOR

Your staff, in any market. Legally and compliantly employed.

An Employer of Record is a third-party service that legally employs international staff on behalf of your business. Marco becomes the entity of record on the employment contract, handling all employer responsibilities while you retain full operational direction over your people.

It is the fastest, most compliant way to hire talent in a country where you have no legal entity and no intention of setting one up yet.

01
Expanding into a new market Test market viability with a local team before committing to a full entity setup.
02
Hiring rare talent across borders Your best candidate is in a country you don't operate in. Hire them anyway.
03
Eliminating entity complexity Skip the months-long setup, legal costs, and ongoing compliance burden of local entity management.
04
Scaling fast across multiple countries Hire across 10 countries simultaneously without building 10 local legal structures.
05
Bridging to your own entity Use EOR as a transitional structure while your permanent entity is being established.
HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. You focus on the hire.

Marco handles everything from legal employment through ongoing HR operations. You stay focused on your business.

STEP 01

You find the talent

Your recruiting team identifies the candidate, agrees on compensation and role scope, and hands the details to Marco. No country restrictions on who you can hire.

STEP 02

Marco hires them

Marco's licensed local entity becomes the legal employer. Employment contract drafted and signed, statutory benefits registered, payroll configured. Typically completed within 5 to 7 working days.

STEP 03

Grow your business

Marco manages payroll, compliance, insurance, time-off, expenses, and visa support on an ongoing basis. You direct the work. Marco handles the employer administration.

THE MARCO DIFFERENCE

EOR is only as good as the entity behind the contract.

Most EOR providers use partner agencies or nominee arrangements in local markets. That means your employees are actually employed by a local third party that Marco or any other aggregator has contracted with. The quality, accountability, and compliance standards vary, often significantly.

Marco's 21 owned entities mean your EOR employees are employed by a Marco entity directly. Same standards. Same platform. Same service team structure. Whether your hire is in Singapore, Romania, or Brazil.

WHAT THIS SERVICE IS

Payroll that runs your way, in every market.

Global Managed Payroll is Marco's core service for multinationals who need consolidated, compliant payroll processing across multiple countries without running separate vendors, teams, or platforms per market.

Marco's model is different from aggregator networks. We operate through owned legal entities in 21 countries, meaning every filing, payment, and statutory contribution runs through a Marco entity. Not a third-party partner. The same platform. The same standards. Every country.

FULL CYCLE COVERAGE

Every component of payroll, handled

From joiner-to-leaver flows through year-end statutory filing. No gaps, no workarounds, no local experts you have to coordinate yourself.

01

Payroll Calendar & Cutoff Management

Pay frequency, pay cycle, change cutoff, pay day, and holiday/weekend rule configuration. Set once, enforced consistently across every entity.

02

Change Data Consolidation

Employee detail updates, bank changes, overtime, absences, pay changes, time-off and expense data consolidated from your HRIS into a single input stream.

03

Joiner & Leaver Processing

Onboarding before the 20th joins the standard payroll cycle. Marco handles social-benefit registration on entry and de-registration on exit. No local chasing required.

04

Payroll Approval Workflow

Maker-Checker-Approver model. Marco prepares and reviews; your Local HR, Regional HR, or designated approver signs off. Full audit trail maintained.

05

Statutory Reporting & Filings

Withholding tax per local tax calendar, social security fund per statutory schedule, and annual tax filing where required. Statutory reports and deduction slips delivered.

06

Payment & Bank-File Services

Bank-file generation and salary disbursement handled by Marco. Payslip format defined at onboarding. Salary and statutory payments processed on schedule, every cycle.

07

GL & Financial Reporting

Payroll-to-GL handover with defined report schedules and approver matrices. GL format aligned to your finance system and integrated with your global ERP.

08

Real-Time BI Dashboards

Headcount changes, workforce cost totals, and cross-country comparisons available live via Marco OS. Progressive activation as each entity goes live.

09

Expat & Shadow Payroll

Shadow, Host, and Split payroll structures for cross-border assignments. Tax-treaty positioning, bilateral reporting, and social-security treatment handled per assignment.

COMPLIANCE & DATA PROTECTION

Built to the standards enterprise requires.

Payroll moves employee financial data across jurisdictions. Marco's compliance framework is designed for enterprises who can't afford exposure.

General Data Protection Regulation

Full data-processing and protection controls for EU and global client data. Privacy-by-design across the Marco OS platform and all in-country operations.

SOC 2 Type 2

Independent third-party audit verification of controls across security, availability, and confidentiality. The enterprise payroll industry benchmark.

ISO 27001:2022

IMPLEMENTATION APPROACH

Phased rollout. Not big-bang.

Large global programs go live in four sequenced phases: pilot, major markets, and full rollout, with BI activation at each stage so visibility grows with coverage.

Phase 01

Scope & Plan

Country and entity headcount defined per phase. Global system integration plan locked. Project governance and stakeholder mapping completed.

Phase 02

Pilot Go-Live

Payroll launched in 3 to 4 pilot countries. Shadow / Host / Split payroll activated for 40 to 50 expats. Headcount-change BI dashboard goes live.

Phase 03

Major Markets

Payroll rolled out across 10 to 12 priority countries. Workforce cost BI activated across regional totals. Process standardization reviewed.

Phase 04

Global Completion

Remaining countries onboarded. Full audit of all live markets. End-to-end program BI activated. Steady-state operations handed over.

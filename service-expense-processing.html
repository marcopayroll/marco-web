SERVICES · EXPENSE PROCESSING

Submit, Approve,
Get paid, That Simple

Global expense management with local tax compliance built in. Marco handles policy setup, billing review, approval workflows, and compliant payment across all your countries so your team stops chasing receipts and starts getting reimbursed on time.

THE PROBLEM

Global expenses are messier than they look.

Employees in different countries submit expenses in different currencies, against different tax rules, with different documentation requirements. What counts as a valid receipt in Germany is not the same as in China or Brazil.

Most companies handle this with spreadsheets, email chains, and a finance team that manually reconciles everything at month end. It is slow, error-prone, and creates real tax exposure when local compliance is missed.

Different rules in every country

Tax treatment, receipt requirements, and benefit-in-kind rules vary by jurisdiction. One global policy does not cover everything.

Manual processes cause delays

Employees wait weeks for reimbursement. Finance teams spend hours reconciling. Approvers do not know what to sign off on.

Tax errors create liability

Wrongly categorized expenses or missed gross-up requirements can trigger audits and back-tax assessments across multiple countries.

No visibility until month end

Finance teams have no real-time view of expense spend. Budget overruns are discovered after the fact, not before.

WHAT WE COVER

Every type of business expense

Marco processes all standard expense categories across all markets, each treated according to the local tax rules that apply to that specific expense type.

Gross-Up Calculations

When tax on an expense must be borne by the employer, Marco calculates the gross-up amount so the employee receives the correct net reimbursement.

Benefit-in-Kind Treatment

Some expenses are taxable benefits in certain countries. Marco identifies and applies the correct benefit-in-kind treatment so reporting stays accurate.

FX and Currency Handling

Expenses submitted in foreign currency are converted at the correct rate for tax reporting purposes. No manual FX reconciliation required from the employee.

Audit-Ready Documentation

Every expense, receipt, approval, and payment stored and indexed in Marco OS. Ready for finance review or tax authority audit at any time.

POLICY DESIGN

One policy framework. Every country covered.

Marco builds your expense policy from the ground up, combining your internal guidelines with local tax rules so the policy works in practice, not just on paper.

01

Country-Specific Rules

Each country's policy reflects local tax law, deductibility rules, and documentation requirements. What is reimbursable in one country may be a taxable benefit in another.

02

Daily limits, per-category caps, and restricted expense types all configured to your company policy. Submissions outside limits flagged automatically.

03

Receipt and Documentation Standards

04

Policy Updates When Rules Change

Tax laws change. When they do, Marco updates your expense policies accordingly. You do not need to monitor every jurisdiction yourself.

THE MARCO DIFFERENCE

Expense compliance is not a software problem. It is a local knowledge problem.

Most expense tools apply the same rules everywhere. That works fine for a single-country business. For a multinational, it creates compliance gaps in every market where the local rules differ from the default.

Marco's local accounting teams review expenses against the actual tax rules in each country. When something does not comply, it gets flagged before payment, not discovered in an audit 18 months later.

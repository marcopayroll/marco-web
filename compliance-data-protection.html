COMPLIANCE

Data Protection
& Privacy

Global payroll means handling some of the most sensitive data in business — employee salaries, personal identifiers, tax records, and financial details across dozens of jurisdictions. Marco treats this responsibility with the rigor it demands.

GDPR Compliant
HIPAA Compliant
SOC 2 TYPE II Audited
ISO 27001:2022 Certified
OUR POSITION

Privacy is not a
compliance checkbox.
It is a commitment.

When a company entrusts Marco with their workforce data, they are trusting us with personal information belonging to real people. That data must be handled with care, accuracy, and discretion.

Marco operates under a privacy-by-design philosophy. Data protection is built into how our systems are architected and our processes run, not applied as an afterthought.

The certifications on this page are independently verified proof that our controls meet standards set by regulators, auditors, and international bodies.

CERTIFICATIONS & FRAMEWORKS

Our compliance
credentials

GDPR

General Data Protection Regulation

EU's primary data protection law governing how personal data is collected, processed, and stored for all EU residents. A baseline requirement across Marco's global client base.

HIPAA

Health Insurance Portability & Accountability Act

Federal standard for protected health information (PHI) for U.S. operations. Marco maintains the administrative, physical, and technical safeguards required under the HIPAA Security Rule.

SOC 2 Type 2

System and Organization Controls Audit

Type 2 evaluates whether controls operated effectively over an extended audit period. An independent auditor verified Marco's controls across security, availability, and confidentiality.

ISO 27001 : 2022

Information Security Management System

International gold standard for information security management. Certification confirms a comprehensive ISMS is in place, covering risk management across people, processes, and technology.

PDPA

Personal Data Protection Act

Thailand's primary data protection law regulating the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data. Compliance is required for Marco's operations involving Thai employees and clients.

PIPL

Personal Information Protection Law

China's comprehensive personal data protection framework governing cross-border data transfers and processing of Chinese residents' information. Applicable to Marco's China operations.

INDIVIDUAL RIGHTS

Rights of employees
whose data we handle

Marco is a data processor acting on behalf of clients. Where regulations grant rights to individuals, we support our clients in fulfilling them.

Right to Access

Individuals can request a copy of their personal data. Applicable under GDPR and across most jurisdictions where Marco operates.

Right to Rectification

Inaccurate or incomplete data can be corrected. Employees raise corrections through their employer.

Right to Erasure

Where no statutory retention requirement exists, individuals may request deletion. Marco processes these on behalf of clients.

Right to Portability

Personal data can be exported in a structured, machine-readable format. Relevant when employees transition between employers or payroll providers.

Right to Object

Individuals may object to certain types of processing. Marco respects these objections within the bounds of legal and contractual obligations.

Right to Restriction

Processing can be restricted in specific circumstances, such as when data accuracy is contested. Procedures are defined in Marco's DPA with each client.
